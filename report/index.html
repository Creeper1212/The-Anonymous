Enhanced Report Submission Process

We've upgraded our reporting system to ensure swift and secure transmission of critical information. All reports are now sent directly to our dedicated team through a secure Discord webhook.

New Feature: Our reports are now processed in real-time through a secure Discord channel, allowing for immediate review and action by our team.

Secure Processing of Your Report

Here's what happens when you submit a report:

  1. Your report is immediately encrypted and sent to our secure Discord channel via webhook.
  2. Our 24/7 moderation team receives an instant notification.
  3. The report is reviewed and triaged based on urgency and severity.
  4. Sensitive information is redacted and stored securely.
  5. Our team initiates the investigation process, collaborating with relevant authorities if necessary.
  6. All actions are logged for accountability and future reference.

Rest assured, your report is handled with the utmost confidentiality and care.
