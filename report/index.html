Secure Processing of Your Report

Here's our process when you submit a report:

  1. Your report is instantly encrypted and securely transmitted to our Discord channel via an encrypted webhook.
  2. Our 24/7 expert moderation team receives an immediate notification with all details.
  3. The report undergoes an initial AI-powered analysis for quick categorization and urgency assessment.
  4. Our team reviews and triages based on AI suggestions, urgency, and severity.
  5. Sensitive information is automatically redacted and stored in our secure, encrypted database.
  6. Our specialized team initiates a thorough investigation process, collaborating with relevant authorities when necessary.
  7. All actions, from submission to resolution, are meticulously logged for accountability and future reference.
  8. You receive an anonymous tracking ID to check the status of your report (if contact information is provided).

Rest assured, your report is handled with the utmost confidentiality, care, and efficiency. Your courage in reporting helps create a safer digital environment for everyone.
